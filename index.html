About The Event

IEEE SB TKMCE as a part of Phase 2 of ENCAMP 2.0, presents JAM, an entertaining session exclusively for the first years.

JAM is an online event in which you will be given a random topic with 5 minutes of preparation and a minute for your presentation.

The best /engaging presentation wins.

So hurry up and showcase your true potential!!!

Time: 9:00 a.m -12 noon.
